Welcome to Remine India!

We are a recognized startup dedicated to recycling e-waste and lithium-ion batteries. Our mission is to create a sustainable future by reducing the environmental impact of electronic waste and promoting responsible disposal.

Our Services

We offer a range of recycling services for electronic waste and lithium-ion batteries, including:

  • E-waste recycling: We recycle all types of electronic waste, including computers, laptops, smartphones, tablets, and more. We ensure that all data is securely erased before recycling.
  • Battery recycling: We specialize in the safe and efficient recycling of lithium-ion batteries, which are commonly found in electronic devices such as laptops, smartphones, and electric vehicles.
  • Pickup services: We offer pickup services for e-waste and lithium-ion batteries from your doorstep, making it easy for you to dispose of them responsibly.

Why Choose Remine India?

  • Environmentally friendly: We are committed to reducing the environmental impact of e-waste and batteries.
  • Secure data destruction: We ensure that all data is securely erased before recycling electronic devices.
  • State-of-the-art technology: We use advanced technology to recover valuable materials from e-waste and lithium-ion batteries.
  • Responsible disposal: We dispose of hazardous materials safely and responsibly.
